About Us

Discover Our Restaurant Story

Ololade Asake is best described as a pan-African restaurant, aimed at promoting the Nigerian and African Cultural heritage through her sumptuous meals. Her mouth-watering smoky-firewood jollof rice has been the gold standard for party jollof and easily won the International Twitter Awards for "Best Jollof" above the likes of Ghanaian Jollof. Almost 20 years on, we continue to serve authentic West African food, staying true to traditional recipes that are eaten across the region. Our dining experiences cater for dish-sharing pleasures, a party of many or one. All this in a modern environment that affords a pleasurable dining experience all-round.
